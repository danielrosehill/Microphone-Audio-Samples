## Features

- Dual MEMS microphones with beamforming
- Yealink Acoustic Shield noise cancellation
- Retractable boom arm for clean look
- Busy light indicator
- USB dongle for PC connectivity
- Certified for Microsoft Teams and UC platforms

## STT Relevance Notes

- **Dual MEMS microphones** with beamforming focus on speaker's voice
- **Noise cancellation** actively reduces background interference
- **Consistent positioning** due to headset form factor
- **Bluetooth connection quality** may vary by dongle/adapter used
- **Two samples recorded** with different Bluetooth adapters for comparison
