## Features

- Dual USB and XLR outputs for versatile connectivity
- Dynamic capsule rejects background noise naturally
- Built-in headphone amplifier for direct monitoring
- Minimal self-noise (no hissing)
- Included accessories: desk stand, mic clip, USB cable, XLR cable

## STT Relevance Notes

- **Cardioid pattern** focuses pickup on sound from the front, rejecting sides and rear
- **Dynamic element** is less sensitive to background noise than condensers
- **50 Hz lower limit** captures full vocal range
- **Low self-noise** improves signal clarity for transcription
- **Plosive sensitivity** - may benefit from pop filter for close-mic use
- **Professional-grade** microphone at entry-level pricing
