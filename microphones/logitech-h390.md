## Features

- Noise-canceling microphone reduces background noise
- Rotating boom arm for flexible positioning
- Padded headband and ear cushions for comfort
- In-line volume and mute controls
- 2-year warranty

## STT Relevance Notes

- **Bi-directional pattern** with noise cancellation helps isolate voice
- **100 Hz - 10 kHz response** is narrower than dedicated mics but covers speech frequencies
- **Consistent mic-to-mouth distance** due to boom arm positioning
- **Budget-friendly** option for basic voice input
- **10 kHz upper limit** may affect clarity of sibilants (s, sh sounds)
