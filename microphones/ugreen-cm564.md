## STT Relevance Notes

- **Omnidirectional pattern** picks up sound from all directions, which may capture more ambient noise
- **100 Hz lower limit** may miss some low-frequency vocal content
- **Budget pricing** makes it accessible for testing
- Best used in quiet environments due to omnidirectional pickup
