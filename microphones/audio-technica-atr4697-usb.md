## Features

- Low-profile design for minimal visibility
- Omnidirectional pickup for full 360° coverage
- Surface-mount (boundary effect) design
- USB bus-powered (no external power needed)
- Lifetime warranty (ATR series)

## STT Relevance Notes

- **Boundary design** uses surface reflection to enhance pickup
- **Omnidirectional pattern** captures room audio from all directions
- **50 Hz lower limit** captures full vocal range
- **Conference-oriented** design optimized for multiple speakers
- **Distance flexibility** - can work at various distances from speaker
- Two samples recorded: close (30cm) and far (80cm) for comparison
